Hi 😄 I am a PhD student majoring biosystems engineering at Seoul National University, under the supervision of Prof. Taehyeong Kim. Prior to my graduate studies, I earned my Bachelor's degree at Chung-Ang University, double-major in Animal Science and Technology and Electrical and Electronic Engineering.
At SNU, my primary research goal is to develop Artificial General Bio-Intelligence. While current AI methodologies are also inspired by biology, their lack of true biological implementation leads to limitations—such as requiring large datasets and massive computational power— especially when compared to human intelligence. To overcome these challenges, I integrate knowledge from animal science, computer science, cognitive science, and mathematics in my research. By designing AI architectures that process complex biological data streams through adaptive learning systems, I strive to contribute to a sustainable, efficient, and inclusive future for everyone.
Additionally, I aim to bridge the gap between the rapid advancements in AI technology and their practical applications within animal science.
